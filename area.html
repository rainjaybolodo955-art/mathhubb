Area Calculations

Find the surface space of standard 2D dimensional shapes.

Rectangle

A = l × w

Square

A = s²

Triangle

A = 0.5 × b × h

Circle

A = πr²

Parallelogram

A = b × h

Trapezoid

A = ((a + b) / 2) × h
